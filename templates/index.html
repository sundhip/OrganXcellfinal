How OrganXcell Works

Simple steps — for everyone, not just doctors

1

Register

Donors and recipients sign up. Medical team verifies your details securely.

2

AI Matches

Our algorithm checks blood group, HLA, urgency, and location to find the best match.

3

Coordinate

Hospital teams get instant alerts. Transport is planned and tracked live on the map.

4

Save a Life

Surgery team is notified. Cold chain is monitored throughout. Every second counts.

🧬

Accurate AI Matching

Organ-specific algorithms — MELD for liver, LAS for lungs, HLA for kidney, urgency tiers for heart. Each match is explained in plain language.

🗺️

Live GPS Tracking

20 hospitals on real map. Transport routes shown live. Cold chain temperatures monitored. Alerts if breach detected.

🤖

Help Chatbot

First-time users get a step-by-step guide. Ask anything — from "what is organ donation" to "how do I check my waiting list position".

📋

Digital Consent

HOTA-compliant family consent workflow. Next-of-kin can sign digitally. Full audit trail kept.

🩸

Blood Bank

Real-time blood group availability. Critical level alerts. Request routing to nearest bank.

📱

Works on Any Device

Simple design that works on phones, tablets, and computers. No training required.

About OrganXcell

Built at Smart India Hackathon 2025 by a team of 6 first-year students. A production-grade platform serving 150+ hospitals.

🎓 SIH 2025

Despite being first-year students, this team built a system with a real PostgreSQL database, WebSocket live tracking, and Claude AI integration.

🐍 Django 5 + DRF + Celery
🗄️ PostgreSQL + Redis
🤖 Claude AI (Anthropic)
🗺️ Leaflet + OpenStreetMap
